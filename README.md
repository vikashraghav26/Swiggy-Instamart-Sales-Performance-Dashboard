# 📊 Swiggy Instamart Sales Performance Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

The Swiggy Instamart Sales Performance Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to analyze grocery delivery sales performance across different shop types, city tiers, and product categories. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales trends, order volumes, customer ratings, and store performance, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

To monitor and evaluate sales performance, customer engagement, and operational efficiency across multiple store formats and geographic regions while identifying growth opportunities and high-performing product categories.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### ✅ Interactive Filters

* Filter data by Year (2021–2024)
* Analyze performance by Shop Size (High, Medium, Small)
* Compare results across City Tiers (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3)

### ✅ Shop Size Performance Analysis

* Visual comparison of sales contribution across different shop sizes.
* Identifies high-performing store formats.

### ✅ City-Wise Sales & Order Analysis

* Compare sales revenue and order volume across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities.
* Understand regional demand patterns and customer behavior.

### ✅ Store Expansion Trend

* Track the number of new store openings from 2021 to 2024.
* Analyze business expansion trends over time.

### ✅ Product Category Performance

* Evaluate sales contribution across major product categories:

  * Fruits & Vegetables
  * Snack Foods
  * Household
  * Frozen Foods
  * Dairy Products
  * Canned Goods
  * Baking Goods
  * Health & Hygiene
  * Meat
  * Soft Drinks

### ✅ Shop Type Performance Summary

* Compare Grocery Stores and Supermarket Types (1, 2, and 3) based on:

  * Total Sales
  * Total Orders
  * Average Rating
  * Average Sales per Store

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 📌 Regional Performance

* Tier 3 cities generated the highest sales revenue (₹472K) and order volume (3.35K), indicating strong demand in emerging markets.

### 📌 Revenue Contribution

* Supermarket Type 1 stores contributed the largest share of total revenue, generating approximately ₹788K in sales.

### 📌 Store Expansion Analysis

* New store openings declined from 2,182 in 2021 to 2,038 in 2024, highlighting a slowdown in physical expansion.

### 📌 Category Performance

* Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods emerged as the top-performing categories, contributing significantly to overall revenue.

### 📌 Customer Satisfaction

* Average customer ratings remained consistently high (3.92–3.93) across all shop formats, reflecting stable service quality.
